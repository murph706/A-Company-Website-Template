Services

Finish Carpentry

We're here to ensure that the interior woodwork of your projects are completed with the finest craftsmenship to give your space a personalized and polished feel. We believe no detail is too small to consider.

Custom Builds

We're experienced creative professionals. We work with clients to design and build anything from the perfect fit built-in shelving to furniture. We pride ourselves on working with reclaimed and repurposed materials that look great and reduce enviormental impact.

Event Staging

We're here to build anything you can imagine. With our exprience building theatrical set pieces, props, and special effect painting, we've created anything from building facades to faux marble steps and platforms. We can craft that stunning centerpiece you and your guests will remember.

Antique Restoration

Let us restore your antiques to their former glory. With care, passion, and ample research for each piece, we bring old and damaged pieces back to life. With training in precision color matching, we're able to repair damaged, highly detailed, painted pieces. We offer pick up and delivery services for your convenience.

Tiling

Looking to add artistic flair to your kitchen or bathroom? Need to replace damaged tiles? Want to redo your tile completely? With a team of professionals who strive to delievery high quality work on a deadline, we've got you covered.

Painting & Refinishing

Want to spruce up your interior woodwork with a coat of paint or stain? We're here to get your interior space looking as good as you envision. We use professional grade, high quailty products, that meet the needs of our clients' budget, ensuring the quality and longevity of our finished product.

About M&M

Welcome to M & M Carpentry and Restoration, where craftsmanship meets creativity. At M & M we are passionate about transforming spaces into works of art through our meticulous attention to detail, innovative designs, and commitment to sustainability. What sets us apart is our dedication to creating not just beautiful spaces, but also functional ones. We understand that every project is unique, which is why we work closely with our clients to tailor solutions that meet their specific needs and lifestyle. From maximizing storage space to optimizing flow and usability, we ensure that every detail is thoughtfully considered and expertly executed. In addition to our passion for design and functionality, we are committed to environmental stewardship. We prioritize the use of sustainable building methods and materials, minimizing our ecological footprint while creating spaces that are healthy for both people and the planet.
